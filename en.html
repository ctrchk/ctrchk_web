About CTRC HK

City Transport Cycle (CTRC) is Hong Kong's dedicated urban cycling platform. We believe cycling is one of the most sustainable and enjoyable ways to move around the city.

Our platform provides carefully planned cycling routes across Hong Kong, from scenic coastal rides to efficient commuter paths. Whether you're a beginner looking for a leisurely weekend ride or an experienced cyclist seeking a challenging route, CTRC has something for you.

Currently focused on Tseung Kwan O, with plans to expand to other districts across Hong Kong.
