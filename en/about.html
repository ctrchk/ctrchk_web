Our Story

"Reduce carbon, starting with me" — not just a slogan, but the founding vision behind City Transport Cycle.

Our Mission: Connect the City, Change the Future

Our mission is to build a comprehensive cycling platform for Hong Kong residents. We are committed to:

  • Promoting cycling: Through easy-to-use route guides and apps, making cycling a part of everyday commuting.
  • Smart services: Drawing on advanced transport systems to provide real-time route navigation and comprehensive riding information.
  • Building a sustainable community: Using gamified achievements and engagement to encourage more people to cycle and work together to reduce the city's carbon footprint.

Our Milestones

2022

The precursor of City Transport Cycle, the "Tseung Kwan O Cycling Light Rail" concept, was born. This was our first attempt to use cycling as an urban transport solution, laying the foundation for the official formation of CTRC.

March 2024

"City Transport Cycle (CTRC)" was officially established. With a grander vision and mission, we began preparing a brand-new cycling platform dedicated to popularising cycling culture in Hong Kong.

May 2024

The first-generation CTRC official website launched. A simple promotional site to introduce our concept and future plans to the public, and to collect initial user feedback.

February 2025

First commuter routes officially launched. We incorporated popular routes such as LOHAS Park to Tseung Kwan O Station into our planning, encouraging residents to use cycling for daily commuting.

October 2025

Expanded service to Sha Tin district, adding multiple new Sha Tin routes. This marked a further expansion of the CTRC service network, covering more areas of Hong Kong.

Expected Q1 2026

Launch of the new CTRC official website and app. This will be a more fully-featured, visually refined platform integrating gamified elements and a membership system.

Future Vision

Potential introduction of a smart bike-sharing service. We will launch a proprietary bicycle card system to provide users with a more convenient and efficient bike rental experience.

Cycling Routes Overview

From the sea breeze of the Tseung Kwan O waterfront to the historic banks of the Sha Tin River, our cycling routes are designed to cover leisure, commuting and challenges to meet the needs of different riders. Every route is carefully planned and field-tested to ensure both safety and enjoyment.
